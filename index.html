How Flip Scout works

The whole system in one page. Tap ? anytime to get back here.

🔍 At a garage sale (under a minute)

  1. Investigate tab: type or dictate the item name (mic button on your keyboard works everywhere).
  2. Tap Investigate, then tap eBay SOLD comps: those are real prices things actually sold for, not asking prices.
  3. Come back, enter your max-buy price and a one-line reason.
  4. Tap Buy or Pass. Both are saved: passes build your price book too.

🧭 The one-sentence map

Investigate = should I buy it? Inventory = I own it. Price Book = what did I decide last time? You can use Inventory's ＋ New item directly for anything you already own; research is optional.

📦 When you buy something

  1. On the buy verdict, tap Acquired: the item form opens pre-filled. Fix the cost to what you actually paid.
  2. It gets a FLIP number. A star (FLIP-0007*) just means the number locks in at next sync.
  3. Set your two prices: quick-sale (move it this week) and patient (willing to wait).
  4. If the girls are in on the deal, add them under Partners: their name, agreed share %, and what cash they put in. The item then shows what each person makes at each price, before you agree.

🛒 Listing and selling

  1. Post the item on FB or eBay yourself (copy helper is coming in the next update).
  2. On the item, tap ＋ Add listing: pick the platform, tap a price tier, done.
  3. When it sells: 💰 Sold… with price, fees, date. Margin and partner payouts compute themselves.
  4. Item didn't work out? Mark dead. It keeps its number and teaches the stats later.

📒 Price book

Middle tab. Search anything you've ever investigated. After a month of scouting you'll make buy calls straight from here without opening a single search.

🔄 The sync pill (top bar)

synced = everything is in your GitHub repo. 2 to sync = captures waiting (normal in dead zones, nothing is lost). Tap the pill to force a sync. Airplane mode is fine: everything saves on the phone and uploads later.

🔑 Sign-in

One token per device, entered once. It lasts about a year and the app warns you 30 days before it expires. Renew at github.com → Settings → Developer settings → Fine-grained tokens.
